foresight

Web Application

Customer

The information technology company from the United Arab Emirates specialized in marketing and advertising that helps fashion brands to increase reputation and loyalty by placing their advertisement alongside highly relevant content on websites.

Objective

Improve the CTR (Click Through Rate) and ROI (Return On Investment) metrics of advertising campaigns for fashion brands by improving the quality of advertisement banners placement and interacting with visual content on websites with a target audience.

Solution

Developed an interactive image format to interact with the target audience. When the user hovers the mouse over the image, the artificial intelligence recognizes the apparel items, colors, shapes and shows similar products available from different brands.
